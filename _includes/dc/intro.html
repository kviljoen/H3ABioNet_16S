The material taught in this module was developed by members of the Computational Biology lab at The University of Cape Town. Its target audience is researchers who have a basic understanding of 16S rRNA gene amplicon sequencing data, who wish to perform a) preprocessing of 16S rRNA amplicon sequencing using a dada2 pipeline implemented in Nextflow and b) downstream analyses in R using an ASV table produced with dada2 as input. Participants will be encouraged to help one another and to apply what they have learned to their own research problems.
